Instructions

How to Use:

  • Fill in project information and work details
  • Capture site photos using your device camera
  • Add multiple rows for dimensions, materials, and manpower as needed
  • Save the report - it's stored on your device
  • Export data periodically to backup and sync with cloud

To Sync to Cloud:

  • Export data as JSON or CSV
  • Upload the file to Google Drive or Dropbox from your device
  • Or email exported reports to project stakeholders
💡 Tip: Install this app on your home screen for quick access. No internet needed on site!
